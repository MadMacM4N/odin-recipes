Bread Machine Cinnamon Rolls

A simple yet delicious version of an old favorite. The bread machine does the

heavy lifting in this recipe. The dough can be made a day in advance and

stored overnight. It is proofed one last time, and ready for the oven.

Nothing like starting the day with the smell of fresh cinnamon rolls filling the

house with their delicious smell!"

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 egg (room temp)
  • 1/4 cup vegatable oil
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • Yeast
    • 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast, or
    • 1 tsp quick rise yeast, or
    • 1 tsp bread machine yeast

    Program Setting: Dough

      Filling:

      • 1/3 cup butter (softened)
      • 1/3 cup brown sugar
      • 2 tbsp cinnamon

      Glaze:

      • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
      • 2 tbsp milk
      • 1/4 tsp vanilla

      Steps:

      1. Once the cycle is complete remove dough and place on a lightly floured surface
      2. Roll into a 12x18 inch rectangle for 18 rolls, (12x24 for 24 rolls) and spread with butter.
      3. Combine remaining ingredients, and sprinkle over butter.
      4. Roll up tightly, jelly-roll style, starting with the longest side and cut into one inch slices. Unwaxed dental floss works well.
      5. Evenly place in a greased baking pan, and let stand in a warm place for 1 hour or until double in size.
      6. Bake at 350F for 25-30 minutes or until done.
      7. Mix glaze ingredients until smooth and drizzle on top.

      Notes:

      Recipe was retrieved from the machines manual in hard copy format.
