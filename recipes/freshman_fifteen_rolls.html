Freshman Fifteen Dinner Roll Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water, plus
  • 1 tbsp water (70-80F)
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 1/2 tsp vegatable oil
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 1/4 cups bread flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 2 tbsp shortening (optional)

Stand Mixer Steps:

  1. Combine all the ingredients, minus the shortening and butter, in the bowl of your stand mixer ni the order listed in the ingredients
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and set aside to rise until the dough has doubled in size, or about one hour.
  3. Once the dough has risen, remove from bowl onto a lightly floured surface. Shape into 15 uniformed sized doughballs. (Use the shortening if the dough is very sticky.
  4. Lightly butter a 9x13 baking pan. Place rolls onto baking pan, cover and let rise until double in size (approx. 30 min.).
  5. Bake in a 350F degree oven for 15 minutes or until a light golden brown.
  6. After removing from the oven, brush with melted butter. Serve warm.

Note:

This recipe can easily be done with a bread machine on the dough setting
